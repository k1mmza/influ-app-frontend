# Persona: Campaign Manager (Agency)

## Profile
- Name: Sarah Chen
- Age: 32
- Role: Senior Campaign Manager
- Company: Digital Marketing Agency
- Experience: 6+ years in influencer marketing

## Goals
- Launch campaigns quickly
- Find high-performing influencers fast
- Manage multiple campaigns efficiently
- Show results clearly to clients

## Pain Points
- Too many manual steps (Excel, DMs, emails)
- Hard to compare influencers objectively
- Influencer data is unreliable (fake followers)
- Slow communication with influencers
- Reporting takes too long

## Needs
- Advanced filtering & discovery tools
- Clear performance metrics (trustable)
- Bulk actions (add multiple influencers)
- Real-time chat + contract system
- Auto-generated reports

## Behavior
- Works fast, low patience
- Data-driven decision maker
- Manages 5–10 campaigns at once

## Key Features Used
- Discover influencers
- Campaign management
- Analytics dashboard
- Export reports

## UX Expectations
- Efficiency > aesthetics
- Minimal clicks
- Clear data visualization
- Keyboard + bulk-friendly actions

## Success Criteria
- Finds best influencers in <10 minutes
- Launch campaign in <1 hour
- Can present results instantly to clients
